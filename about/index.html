Mark Bradshaw
Ph.D. Mechanical Engineering, Boston University 2013

B.S. Mechanical Engineering, University of New Hampshire 2004

Mark Bradshaw works as an engineer at Atkinson Thin Film Systems Inc. developing applications of sputtered thin films. His interests are in Thin films, as well as the influence of the cell environment on cell behavior.
